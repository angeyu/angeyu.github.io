GoodDeed: A User Case Study

Let's make community involvement easier than ever!

Background

Over 90 percent of Americans want to volunteer but only one out of four individuals actually do*. We want to find out what’s stopping people from volunteering and to create GoodDeed, an app to remove these barriers.

Our 5-person team conducted a 3-phase case study over the course of 6 weeks to understand how people typically find and participate in volunteering opportunities.
